	
	Chapter 0: Prologue and Tutorial

This is the tutorial chapter.
It starts at the port in the town and ends at the entrance to Strahnbrad.
Goals of this chapter:
  o Teach the game to the players
    > Attack an ally to interact with them
    > Pledge to an altar
    > Buy a talent
    > You can buy units from barracks

    Plot
You arrive on a ship from an unknown place.
You were supposed to meet Arthas here, but he left for Strahnbrad.
Make your way to Strahnbrad through Kings Road.
	
1. You have to meet Arthas at the entrance to Strahnbrad
