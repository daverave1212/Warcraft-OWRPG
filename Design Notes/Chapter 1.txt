
	Chapter 1 : The Defense of Strahnbrad
The chapter starts at Uther's camp and ends at the end of Strahnbrad.

0. Cinematic
1. Arthas orders you to recruit 6 more soldiers from the village.
Quick fade-in/fade-out and Arthas disappears.
2. Objective: Talk to up to 6 Willing Villagers and recruit them.
When recruiting, fade-in/fade-out and they disappear, then appear around Arthas.
Villager Positions:
	o Right outside the camp
	o In a gnoll cage, east
	o Being attacked by a wendingo, south
	o 2 captured by bandits, south-east
	o In the clearing in the middle of the map
